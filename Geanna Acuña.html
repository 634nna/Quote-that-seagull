Unbounded

Here and now, the air runs free

Like you, who longs to be.

No mask to wear, no roles to play

Only you, you see.


Your truth unfolds, a quiet flame,

No need for praise, no need for fame.

The world bends softly to your song,

A place where you have always belonged.


Be as you are, no less, no more

A sea of you in every shore

This moment calls, lighting your way

You are yourself, come what may.
