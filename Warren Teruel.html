Freedom's Flight

Rules chain the heart,

anchors dragging wings to the earth.

They whisper of obedience,

of paths carved by those who fear the sky.


But what is law,

if not the pulse of the wind

urging us higher?


Freedom is the law I follow,

its voice a whisper of clouds,

its promise a limitless horizon.


Each beat of my wings

defies gravity’s grasp,

each breath tastes of the infinite.


I am not bound by what they decree.

I am bound only

by the call of the open air.
